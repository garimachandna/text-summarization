Coronavirus | Over 2,000 COVID-19 deaths on single day
For the first time, more than 2,000 COVID-19 deaths were registered in a single day in India (without considering backlogs) on Tuesday (April 20). As many as 2,007 deaths and 2,93,534 cases were recorded in the country.Maharashtra reported 62,097 infections (accounting for nearly 22% of the new cases) on Tuesday, followed by Uttar Pradesh with 29,574 new infections and Delhi with 28,395 new cases.As many as 15,19,486 samples were tested on Monday (results of which were made available on Tuesday). This was only the second instance when the number of daily tests crossed the 15-lakh-mark. Over 15.66 lakh tests were conducted on April 17.
