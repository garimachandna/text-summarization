COVID News Updates: UK detects two cases of new COVID-19 variant 'Omicron'; four more African countries added to Britain's travel red list


The UK on Saturday detected two cases of the new COVID-19 variant, Omicron, according to health secretary Sajid Javid.The patients were detected in Chelmsford and Nottingham. The new variant has also been identified in South Africa, Botswana, Belgium, Hong Kong and Israel.
