Novel Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) Situation Update Report - 44

Ministry of Home Affairs issues revised guidelines for surveillance, containment and caution.
Prime Minister chairs high level meeting with Chief Ministers of Haryana, Delhi, Chhattisgarh, Kerala, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Gujarat, and West Bengal to review status and preparedness of COVID-19 response and management.
Prime Minister reviewed preparedness of COVID-19 vaccine delivery, distribution, and administration.
Union Health Minister calls for continued focus on the goal to eliminate TB by 2025 through advocacy and engagement especially at a time when COVID-19 is taking prioritized attention.
Minister of Health ensures that the COVIN portal and network will assure last mile vaccine delivery.
Union Health Minister calls upon young scientists from Shanghai Cooperation Organization to collaborate to develop solutions for COVID19 and common societal challenges.
