Americans still face struggles despite trillions in coronavirus aid

As Congress prepares another injection of COVID-19 aid for businesses and individuals, there’s been debate about whether it’s necessary on top of the US$3.5 trillion spent so far. Despite trillions of dollars in government assistance, about two-thirds of families that suffered job losses or reduced wages during the pandemic still reported facing serious financial hardship. Many people were struggling – and still are – just to pay for basic necessities, like food and rent.
